Entering the letter followed by a period means the letter is in the right spot.

Entering the letter followed by a comma means it's in the wrong spot.

Entering the letter on its own means the letter is not in the answer.

E.g. A,ROSE. means that R,O,S are not in the word, E is in the right place, and A is in the wrong spot.
